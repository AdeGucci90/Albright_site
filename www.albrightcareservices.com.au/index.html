    About

    US

    Albright Care Services Pty Ltd provides disability care services by assisting the participants to achieve their goals. Importantly, we partner with our participants to meet their support needs. Our target age group for participants is 7 and 65 years of age. Our point of difference is that we only employ workers who are highly experienced and are ready to treat our participants with respect and love as they would their own family members.

    At Albright Care Services Pty Ltd, we strive to improve the quality of life and promote more independence in those receiving NDIS. We know that everyone is unique, with different needs, goals, and aspirations so we tailor our NDIS services to suit these.

    Our

    SERVICES

    ALBRIGHT CARE SERVICES PTY LTD offers support (under NDIS) to the following registration groups:

    Development of Daily Living and Life Skills 

    At Albright Care Services Pty Ltd we can assist you to increase your ability to live independently by learning daily life skills. These basic life skills will not only help You be independent but will also improve You lifestyle as well as Your physical and mental well-being.

    Assist Personal Activities

    We offer services to those participants who are living in their own homes or supported accommodations such as personal care supports relating to assistance with Daily Personal Activities, including assistance with, or supervision of, Personal Tasks of Daily Life, including toileting, personal hygiene, dressing and grooming, medicine intake, attending appointments, mobility and transferring, use of aids and appliances, eating and drinking. 

    Assist Daily Tasks/Shared Living

    Albright Care Services Pty Ltd have a team of compassionate and qualified support workers who can do your daily tasks for you or assist you in performing tasks such as Meal Preparation, Games and Fun Activities, Grocery Shopping for Home, Supported Independent Living, Individualised Living Options and Drop-in Supports for Shared Living.

    Household Tasks

    At ALBRIGHT CARE SERVICES PTY LTD we know that daily household tasks can be difficult to perform for many people with disabilities such as limited mobility or cognitive disability. They would require assistance for some of the tasks that they cannot manage.

    Participate in Community, Social and Civic Activities

    One of the main aspects of NDIS is to assist participants to engage with the community by taking part in a range of activities. There is an array of activities that people with disabilities can engage in that can enrich their lives. 

    Assist-Life Stages, Transition and Support

    At Albright Care Services Pty Ltd we understand that sometimes it is difficult to adapt to something new or to move into a new how. These are huge changes to your life. These can be especially difficult for people with disabilities as they will need to change their routines to adapt to their new life

    Assist Travel/Transport

    A core objective for ALBRIGHT CARE SERVICES PTY LTD is to assist our participants to remain connected to their communities by enabling them to participate in social activities and events and by assisting them tp accomplish everyday undertakings for daily living.

    Innovative Community Participation

    At Albright Care Services Pty Ltd we assist participants to engage in activities that will allow them to build all the necessary skills they require to participate in the community- actively.

    At Albright Care Services, we strive to improve quality of life and promote more independence for those receiving NDIS. We know that everyone is unique, with different needs, goals, and aspirations – that’s why we tailor our NDIS services.

    YOUR Rights

    Albright Care Services PTY LTD Consumer Rights

    As an individual, you have many rights. We support and assist you in identifying and exercising these rights to achieve your goals. ALBRIGHT CARE SERVICES PTY LTD adopts a policy of non-discrimination regarding eligibility and entry to our services, and when providing support services to you.

    You have the right to:

    • Access supports that promote, uphold, and respect your legal and human rights.

    • Exercise informed choice and control to maximize independence

    • Freedom of expression, self-determination, and personal decision-making.

    • Access supports that respect your culture, diversity, values, and beliefs.

    • A support service that respects your right to privacy and dignity

    • Be supported to make informed choices that will maximize independence.

    • Receive support that is free from violence, abuse, neglect, exploitation or discrimination.

    • Receive supports which are overseen by strong operational management.

    • Receive services that are safeguarded by informed and compliant risk and incident management systems.

    • Advise consent to the sharing of information between providers during the transition.

    • Opt-out of giving information as required by NDIS.
